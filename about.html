About Us

Post Production Liquidators was founded to solve a critical, exhausting bottleneck in the modern film production lifecycle: wrap-week logistics. We watched overworked production office coordinators and wardrobe crews spend precious final wrap hours tracking down boxes, managing heavy lifting all while trying to hit a rigid soundstage lease deadline. We stepped in to turn that chaos into a single, seamless process.

Backed by years of hands-on experience navigating high-volume secondary apparel markets, garment asset valuation, and large-scale inventory liquidation, our team built a specialized framework designed specifically for the entertainment industry. We don't just move clothing; we manage the liability, privacy, and speed requirements unique to major studio environments.

Based proudly in the heart of the Atlanta film hub, we are built to rapidly deploy directly to soundstages across Georgia. Our mission is simple: protect your crew's hours, safeguard your studio's intellectual property, and ensure your space is left clean on your vacate date.
